How It Works

TUMUGI creates a Union where multiple servers can connect and talk together in real-time.

Add TUMUGI

Invite the bot to your Discord server.

/union join

Join your server to a Union.

/vc connect

Connect to a voice channel in the Union.

Talk Together

Command Guide

Simple commands to connect your world.

/union join [union_name]

Join a Union with the specified name.

Requires "Manage Server" permission.

/union leave

Leave your current Union.

Requires "Manage Server" permission.

/vc connect [union_name]

Connect to a voice channel in the Union.

Requires "VC Connect" permission.

/vc disconnect

Disconnect from the voice channel.

Requires "VC Disconnect" permission.
